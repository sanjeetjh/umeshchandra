Farewell by the Cuddapah People


Umeshchandra is given a farewell unparalleled in the history of Cuddapah. This video clip also shows the fondness that Umeshchandra had for the people of Cuddapah. The people of Cuddapah make clear their adoration for Umeshchandra.

Many of Umeshchandra's colleagues express their admiration, adoration and affection for him. He was given the title "Cuddapah Tiger".
